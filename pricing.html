Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.
Do you offer student discounts?
Absolutely! Students get 50% off any plan with a valid .edu email address. Contact support to apply your discount.
Is there a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card required to start your trial.
What happens to my portfolio if I cancel?
Your portfolio remains active for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your data during this period.
Can I use my own domain?
Yes! All plans include custom domain support. We'll help you connect your existing domain or register a new one.
Is technical support included?
Yes, all plans include support. Starter gets email support, Professional gets priority support, and Agency+ gets phone support.
